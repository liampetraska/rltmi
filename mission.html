Mission Statement

RLT is a compassionate and knowledgeable retirement and estate planning company offering the most innovative retirement solutions along with specialized trust services. We are proud to partner with and represent only the strongest companies and leading experts in the industry. We strive to become recognized as one of Michigan’s leading retirement and estate planning companies, through serving the large numbers of people without retirement and estate plans. Our goal is to ensure that RLT clients and their families are financially prepared and protected for a lifetime and longer.
