our team

RLT has partnered with top attorney's and tax advisor's that specialize in "Retirement and Estate Planning". Our team proudly serves the entire state of Michigan and they are the leaders in their fields of expertise. Take this time to learn a little more about who we are.


Ron Thompson

Ron Thompson is the founder of RLT. Strategic, thorough and caring, Ron has always had a head for numbers and a good neighbor attitude. One client said, “…until we worked with Ron, we didn’t understand our revocable trust. He took the time to teach us about it and made sure we understood each feature. What a difference that made.” Among industry peers, Ron is warmly referred to as “RT3.” Ron’s favorite past times when not working hard to figure out winning solutions for his clients are golfing, attending his son’s sports and activities, watching Spartan and Notre Dame Football and of course, the Detroit Red Wings.



Lisa Thompson

Lisa Thompson is an associate consultant at RLT. Business-minded, with a strong work-ethic, she is described as being wise, caring and inspiring. One of her favorite sayings is “One generation plants the trees, another one gets the shade.” Lisa shares this philosophy while working with RLT clients and their families and in everything else she does. She has helped initiate a number of community-improvement projects over the years, many of which are on-going. Raymond T. Rowe, P.C.

Shawn Weera, Attorney at Law
